SOME GREAT PERSONALITIES

Mahatma Gandhi

Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi or just simply Mahatma Gandhi was born on 2 October 1869. He was an Indian lawyer and a nationalist too.Born and raised in a Hindu family in coastal Gujarat, western India, Gandhi trained in law at the Inner Temple, London, and was called to the bar at age 22 in June 1891. After two uncertain years in India, where he was unable to start a successful law practice, he moved to South Africa in 1893 to represent an Indian merchant in a lawsuit. He went on to stay for 21 years.His role to fight for independence was very much. He was leader of some of the most effective and important movements like Salt movement and Simon Go back. At last he died at the age of 79 years on 30 January 1948. And Gandhiji's birthday is celebrated as Gandhi Jayanti in our country every year on 2 October.
